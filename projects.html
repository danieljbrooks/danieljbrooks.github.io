Projects

Draftsim

A popular website which allows you to practice drafting Magic: the Gathering against smart computer opponents. The site also features pick recommendations and automatic deckbuilding. A description of the draftsim model can be found here.

Polymer Simulations

A workflow for running molecular dynamics simulations on polymer systems. This method has been applied to study materials for energy storage and artificial photosynthesis.

Dendritic Growth

Lithium dendrites pose a safety hazard in lithium-ion batteries. Here, a model is developed to demonstrate how pulsed charging can inhibit their formation.
