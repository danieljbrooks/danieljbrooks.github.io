Homepage

I recently defended my PhD in Applied Physics from Caltech with a thesis on computational materials design. Previously, I graduated from Cornell University with a B.S. in Engineering Physics, Summa Cum Laude.

Thie website highlights some of the projects and publications that I've worked on. There are also a number of blog posts on topics that I think other people might find useful.

My research interest is primarily in understanding the mathematical underpinnings of complex systems. More generally, I am interested in a broad range of problems at the intersection of software engineering, physics and data science.
